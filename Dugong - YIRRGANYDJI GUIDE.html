Conservation Efforts

Dugongs  overfishing and, environmental change. Conservation efforts such as spreading awareness these animals hold to the environment and indigenous communitas are present, however they must reach more of the population.

Significance To The Yiraganji People

 Dugongs play a significant spiritual and practical role in the Yirrigandji community, acting as a protein rich food source and a tradition medicine. Dugongs are commonly mentioned in dreamtime stories, serving as totems regarding the evolution of animalia of the land.

Fun Facts

Dugongs (Duyun) also known as "sea cows", are more closely related to elephants than Wales. 
Dugongs are also one of the few marine mammals that eat only plants, With there Diet primarily composed of sea grass.
